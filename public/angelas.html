Angela's Recipe for the best Cinnamom Roll

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • ¾cup warm milk
  • 2½teaspoon yeast
  • 3½teaspoon sugar
  • 1 cuo melted butter
  • 2 cups all purpose flour

For the filling

  • ⅔brown sugar or jaggery
  • 1¼cinnamom powder
  • 1 cup melted chocolate
  • 2 cup oil or Butter

Instructions

  1. Mix milk with yeast,eggs,sugar
  2. melt butter and add in flour
  3. Add flour and combine to get adough
  4. Knead the dough and keep for rest uupto 10 mins
  5. Transfer the dough in big bowl 7 keep for 2 hrs
  6. After doubled in size cut and roll in rectangle shape
  7. Melt butter and sugar with cinnamom powder and apply on the roll
  8. Place the rolls in baking tray and bake at 300 degree
  9. preheat till 20 to 30 mins untill it gets brown colour and the serve the dish
